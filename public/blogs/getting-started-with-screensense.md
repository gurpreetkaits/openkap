Welcome to OpenKap! This guide will help you create your first screen recording and share it with your team.

## Why Screen Recording?

Screen recordings are one of the most effective ways to communicate complex ideas. Whether you're:

- **Explaining a bug** to your development team
- **Creating a tutorial** for new team members
- **Giving feedback** on a design mockup
- **Documenting a process** for future reference

A quick screen recording can save hours of back-and-forth communication.

## Step 1: Log In to OpenKap

Navigate to the OpenKap application and log in with your Google account. We use Google OAuth for secure, passwordless authentication.

## Step 2: Start Recording

Once logged in, you'll see the **Record** button in the sidebar. Click it to open the recording panel.

You can choose to record:

1. **Your entire screen** - Great for tutorials and walkthroughs
2. **A specific window** - Perfect for app demos
3. **A browser tab** - Ideal for web-based content

## Step 3: Record Your Content

Click **Start Recording** and begin your demonstration. A few tips:

> **Pro Tip:** Speak clearly and at a moderate pace. You can always re-record if needed!

- Keep recordings concise when possible
- Use your cursor to point at important elements
- Pause briefly before clicking to let viewers follow along

## Step 4: Stop and Review

When you're done, click **Stop Recording**. Your video will be automatically processed and optimized for streaming.

You can:
- Preview your recording
- Add a title and description
- Trim the beginning or end

## Step 5: Share Your Recording

Every recording gets a unique share link. Click the **Share** button to:

- Copy the link to your clipboard
- Share directly to Slack or Discord
- Embed the video in documentation

## What's Next?

Now that you've created your first recording, explore these features:

- **Comments** - Team members can leave timestamped comments
- **Reactions** - Quick feedback with emoji reactions
- **Favorites** - Save important recordings for easy access
- **HLS Streaming** - Adaptive quality for any connection
