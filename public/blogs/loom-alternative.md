If you've been using screen recording tools and feel like they've become bloated with features you don't need, ScreenSense might be worth a look.

## What is ScreenSense?

ScreenSense is a screen recording tool built with one goal: **record and share quickly**. No complex dashboards, no feature overload—just the essentials done well.

## What You Get

### Recording

- **Screen + audio capture** in one click
- **Resolution options**: 720p, 1080p, or 4K
- Record your entire screen or a specific window

### Sharing

- **Instant shareable links** — available immediately, even before processing finishes
- **Token-based secure URLs** — 64-character random tokens for each video
- **Optional expiration dates** for time-sensitive content
- **Public/private toggle** for access control

### Streaming

- **HLS adaptive streaming** — video quality adjusts to viewer's connection
- Smooth playback without buffering issues
- Works across all modern browsers

### Engagement

- **Timestamped comments** — viewers can leave feedback at specific moments
- **Reactions** — quick feedback with 👍 ❤️ 🔥 👏 🤔
- No account required for viewers to react

### Analytics

- Total views and unique viewer count
- Average watch duration
- Completion rate (how many watched the full video)
- Recent viewers list
