Pricing for software shouldn't require a spreadsheet to understand. Here's how OpenKap pricing works.

## Our Pricing

### Free Plan — $0

- **1 video** stored
- **Unlimited duration**
- All features included (sharing, comments, reactions, analytics)

Good for trying things out or occasional use.

### Pro Monthly — $7/month

- **Unlimited recordings**
- **Unlimited duration** per recording
- Priority support

### Pro Yearly — $80/year

- Same as Pro Monthly
- Works out to **~$6.67/month**
- Save by paying annually

## What's Included in Pro

Everything. There's no "Business" tier or "Enterprise" add-ons. When you pay $7/month, you get:

- Unlimited screen recordings
- Unlimited recording duration
- HLS adaptive streaming
- Instant shareable links
- Timestamped comments
- Viewer reactions
- View analytics (total views, unique viewers, watch duration, completion rate)
- Secure token-based sharing
- Expiration dates for links
- 720p, 1080p, and 4K recording support
- Priority support

## No Per-Seat Pricing

You pay one price. Whether you're a solo founder or sharing videos with a team of 50, the cost is the same: $7/month.

Your viewers don't need accounts. They can watch, comment, and react without signing up.

## No Hidden Fees

- No storage fees
- No bandwidth charges
- No "premium features" locked behind higher tiers
- No annual contract required (monthly billing available)

## How This Compares

Most screen recording tools charge per user, which adds up quickly for teams. A 10-person team on typical per-seat pricing can easily cost $150-200/month.

With OpenKap: **$7/month total**.

We keep costs low by:
- Focusing on essential features instead of building everything
- Using efficient streaming technology (HLS)
- Being open source (community contributions help)

## Try Before You Pay

The free plan lets you test everything. Record a video, share it, see the analytics. If it works for you, upgrade. If not, no hard feelings.
