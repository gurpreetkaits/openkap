## Built for Real-World Network Conditions

### HLS Adaptive Streaming

Internet speeds vary everywhere—fast fiber in some areas, slower connections in others. OpenKap uses HLS (HTTP Live Streaming) which automatically adjusts video quality based on the viewer's connection.

- Fast connection? Full quality.
- Slow connection? Lower resolution that actually plays smoothly.

No buffering wheels. No "video failed to load" errors.

### Instant Sharing

Share links are available immediately after recording—even before the video finishes processing. This is useful when you need to quickly share something with a client or teammate in a different timezone.
